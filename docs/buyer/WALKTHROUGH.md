# Buyer Walkthrough

The included `docs/screenshots/release-2026-07-13/` images and [recorded walkthrough](../screenshots/release-2026-07-13/guided-demo-walkthrough.webm) demonstrate the product without relying on a live AI request.

1. Open the landing page and choose **Open guided demo**.
2. Review source material and the structured workflow canvas.
3. Run **Validate** to surface structural readiness checks.
4. Choose **Generate**, review the composed deliverable, then export Markdown, PDF, or Project JSON.
5. Open **Data portability** to show project export, full browser backup, staged restore, and scoped clearing.
6. On mobile, use **Inspector** and **Snapshots** to open the dedicated sheets.

The standard flow is browser-local. Optional provider calls require a user-entered key and an explicit per-request consent confirmation.
