# WeaveStudio Excluded Assets and Retained Rights

> Draft transaction schedule for buyer diligence and legal review. The signed purchase agreement controls if it differs from this document.

## Excluded personal accounts and credentials

The following are not included in the acquisition:

- The seller's GitHub account, Vercel account, Google account, Gmail account, payment accounts, password manager, developer accounts, or any other personal account.
- Passwords, API keys, access tokens, recovery codes, SSH keys, signing keys, environment files, browser profiles, or authentication sessions.
- The seller's computer, local filesystem, local development tools, editor settings, operating-system configuration, or personal backup media.
- Personal contact information except the limited business contact information intentionally included in the signed transaction documents.

A repository or Vercel project may be transferred to a buyer-owned account. The underlying seller account is never part of the transaction.

## Excluded products and intellectual property

The acquisition does not include:

- QuoteForge Local, RedactReady Local, VariantVision, Amino Acids Research Workbench, JournalFlow, IHOS, BuildWorld AI, Garden Grid Planner, or any other seller project not expressly listed in the signed asset schedule.
- Generic skills, know-how, methods, ideas, development techniques, or experience that remain part of the seller's general professional capability.
- Third-party libraries, frameworks, fonts, icons, services, trademarks, or other material that the seller does not own. Those items remain governed by their respective licenses and terms.
- Patent rights or patent applications unless expressly listed. No patent filing or patent-clearance claim is included.

## Excluded business assets and claims

WeaveStudio is offered as a software and IP asset, not as an operating business. The acquisition does not include:

- Customers, customer contracts, subscribers, mailing lists, user accounts, or active-user records.
- Revenue, receivables, subscriptions, renewal rights, financial statements, or a claim of product-market fit.
- Employees, contractors, vendors, offices, bank accounts, insurance, licenses, regulatory approvals, or business entities.
- Compliance certifications, security certifications, legal opinions, regulated-workflow approvals, warranties of market success, or guaranteed future performance.
- A custom domain. The current public deployments use Vercel-provided domains unless a separately documented domain is later added to the signed asset schedule.

## Excluded services and future work

The acquisition does not include:

- Unlimited support, maintenance, hosting, monitoring, product management, sales assistance, marketing, or business-development services.
- New features, custom integrations, cloud synchronization, authentication, collaboration, billing, migration of buyer data, or compatibility work not expressly included in the signed agreement.
- Continuing access to the buyer's repository or hosting accounts after the agreed support period.
- Any obligation to achieve a particular revenue, user, conversion, financing, acquisition, or commercialization outcome.

## Seller's retained archival and attribution rights

Unless the signed agreement states otherwise, the seller retains a narrow right to:

- Keep private archival copies solely for legal, tax, evidentiary, and record-retention purposes.
- Identify himself as WeaveStudio's original creator in a résumé, portfolio, interview, case study, or historical project list.
- Display screenshots and publicly disclosed information that were already public before closing, provided the seller does not distribute source code, confidential buyer information, or represent continued ownership.

These retained rights do not permit the seller to resell, commercially relaunch, sublicense, or distribute the transferred WeaveStudio source or exclusive product assets after closing.

## No implied transfer

Any asset not expressly included in the signed agreement and final asset schedule remains excluded.
