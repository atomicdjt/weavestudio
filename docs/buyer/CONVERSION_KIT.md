## Transfer checklist

1. Signed asset-purchase agreement identifying source, brand, domains, accounts, repositories, documentation, and exclusions.
2. Buyer validates the acquisition ZIP checksum and installs from clean documentation.
3. Transfer GitHub repository ownership/access and rotate/revoke seller credentials.
4. Transfer or reconfigure the dedicated Vercel demo project and any domain only after buyer control is confirmed.
5. Buyer creates fresh provider, email, database, analytics, and billing credentials; do not transfer personal keys.
6. Provide the agreed handover window and record completion.
